About diana

My name is Diana. I enjoy making banana bread and taking care of my plants. I started this website because I wanted to post my poems somewhere simple, but it ended up being a place to my current projects, interests and aspirations. My last adventure was participating on the National Poetry Writing Month, the annual poem writing challenge and hearing dharma talks of a 14 day online meditation retreat.

Usually, you'll find me...

  • Hearing a new Waking up series
  • Double checking ingredients for a new recipe
  • Waiting for the sun to come down to do some gardening

First three things I do when I wake up

  2. Smooch my partner
  3. Think how I want to oversleep
